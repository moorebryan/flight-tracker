Which Clustering Algorithm?

K-Means clustering has been chosen due to its ability to define the number of clusters and it was shown to be relatively good when examining clustering error metrics.

Why Only 5 Clusters?

Due to limitations in heroku it cannot calculate and display the number of clusters I want to show (23). I thus am showing the highest possible number allowed while including 100 flights per day. Any more crashes the server.

Future Plans

In the future I will crawl along each individual fligt and find which waypoints it passess near to. Using that I will use K-Nearest Neighbors to assign each flight the most likely trajectory.
